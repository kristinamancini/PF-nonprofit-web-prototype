OUR MISSION

The Piatigorsky Foundation shares live classical music with communities throughout the US.
Gregor Piatigorsky, the renowned cellist, believed that music is not a luxury for an elite few, but a necessity of life for all. Our community concerts bring together world-class musicians and eager audiences to celebrate the joy of music.
